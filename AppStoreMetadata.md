## Review Prompt Strategy (Implemented)

**Trigger conditions:**
- After 2+ successful "good" operations
- Good = compression with >15% reduction, OR any merge, OR any split
- Subsequent prompts only after 4+ months

**When prompted:**
- When user taps "Done" on success screen
- 1.5 second delay to let UI settle

**Never prompt:**
- During an operation
- After a failed operation
- More than ~3 times per year (Apple rate-limits)
